How to use this tool

This tool helps you find your BIS for Leagues 6. Select the two stats you want to maximise in the top-right corner (e.g. Mage Acc vs Total Def for water thorns).

Below the graph, there's a table for each slot. This shows the items that are situationally BIS. No other item is better in both of your selected stats. To improve one stat, you must lose out on the other.

The weapon slot is excluded, because you probably know what weapon you're going to use.

If an item is unobtainable in your regions (or you just don't have it yet), click the X.

The graph shows all the viable combinations of those items. Up-and-to-the-right is better. Click a node to see the build.
